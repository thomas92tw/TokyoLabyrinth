# Initial Concept
A creative and technical environment for developing literary projects, automation tools, and integrated visual content.

# Product Guide - Creative Workspace

## Target Users
*   **Content Creators and Writers**: Individuals focused on generating and managing structured literary works (e.g., "Tokyo Labyrinth").

## Primary Goals
*   Provide a robust and organized environment for managing, generating, and polishing creative literary content from inception to final draft.

## Key Features
*   **Automated Visual Integration**: Seamless integration with high-fidelity image generation tools to create consistent book covers, chapter illustrations, and social media assets that match the narrative tone.

## Success Metrics
*   **Aesthetic Cohesion**: Measured by the visual consistency and artistic quality of the generated illustrations across all chapters and promotional materials.
