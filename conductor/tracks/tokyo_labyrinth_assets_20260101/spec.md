# Track Spec: Organize and Validate "Tokyo Labyrinth" Project Assets

## Overview
This track focuses on the formal organization and validation of the existing "Tokyo Labyrinth" (東京迷途) project assets. It ensures all manuscripts and images are standardized and meet the established quality criteria.

## Objectives
1.  **Structure Standardization**: Verify the directory structure (Manuscripts, Images, Social).
2.  **Manuscript Validation**: Confirm all 13 chapters (including Foreword and Epilogue) are present and meet the ~1200 word Traditional Chinese requirement.
3.  **Asset Integrity**: Verify all 13 high-fidelity images are correctly named and located.
4.  **Metadata Alignment**: Ensure the Table of Contents (TOC) and Thread summaries match the final manuscript content.

## Acceptance Criteria
- All 13 `.md` files exist in `Desktop/創作靈感/文稿/`.
- All 13 `.png` files exist in `Desktop/創作靈感/配圖/`.
- Word count for each chapter is verified to be approximately 1200 Traditional Chinese characters.
- `東京迷途_目錄.md` correctly references all chapters.
